Maureen Bockrath

Hi I’m Maureen. I’m a Hufflepuff from California. I have a cat named Jag and two and a half dogs. I work for a non-profit second hand store that raises money for local mental health agencies. I design postcards for specific issues to send your elected representatives.

I love books (Turtles All the Way Down and Truly Devious), TV (Runawys, The West Wing and Outlander at the moment), movies, musicals (Hamilton right now) and comics (mostly Marvel, because who has time for more than one?).

My favorite color is dark purple. My favorite kind of pizza is pepperoni and black olive. My favorite superhero is Wiccan. I’ve actually seen Hamilton. .
